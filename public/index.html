About us

VC4ALL is a messaging and video conferencing tool developed by Careator Technologies Pvt. Ltd., in 2018. VC4ALL team is led by three visionaries Aditya Damuluri (CEO & Founder), Sushil Kumar (Partner and Technology Architect), Logeshwari Gurusamy (Partner and Technology Architect). This tool is a conscious attempt to help children and their parents get connected to the school teachers instantly. VC4ALL helps the parents have a dedicated one-to-one discussion with the teachers without having to travel down to the respective school. Not only this, but VC4ALL also lets teachers and parents have a group conversations to have a better perspective about the children’s overall growth. This uninterrupted connectivity allows the schools to let the children get education over live chats and video calls.
