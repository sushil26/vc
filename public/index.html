About us

VC4ALL is a holistic new age technology platform for schools integrated with messaging and video conferencing.

The genesis of VC4All is to ease the communication between Parent and Teacher, and get connected anywhere. VC4ALL helps the parents to have a dedicated one-to-one discussion with the teachers without having to travel down to the respective school. Not only this, but VC4ALL also lets teachers and parents have a group conversations to have a better perspective about the children’s overall growth.

This uninterrupted connectivity allows the schools to let the children get education over live chats and video calls.
